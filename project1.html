uh oh, a goblin stole your brain

Date: June 2024

Designed, scripted, animated, and edited a fun little animatic for final capstone project. Uh oh, a goblin stole your brain, or Brain Goblin, is a short animatic I created for my final capstone project at Clark College. I chose this project to challenge and advance my animation skills. More than that, this project was confirmation of my desire and drive to pursue the creative arts. I learned so much from this experience and will use it to further aid my growth in the future.
