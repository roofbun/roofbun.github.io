Wrong Way

Date: November 2022

A class assignment to make an image montage. For this project we were required to make a montage that seamlessly integrated different images into one dreamscape scene. The idea I chose to pursue was a person pushing forward to some sort of goal, despite all of the signs telling them not to. Through my sketches I refined the idea, eventually settling on this staircase with tree-like signs looming over it. I played a lot with the lighting in order to highlight the most important elements.
